Hi, I'm Rahul!

I started CodingHitoHai because I remember how confusing it felt when I first tried to learn programming. Every tutorial assumed too much. Every book had too much jargon. I wanted to change that.

I've been coding for 7+ years, working with Java, Python, SQL, and web technologies. But more importantly, I've helped hundreds of beginners go from zero to writing their very first program — with a smile!

My teaching style is simple: no complicated words, lots of examples, and always beginner-first. Whether you're a student, a professional looking to switch careers, or just someone curious about coding — you're welcome here! 🙌
